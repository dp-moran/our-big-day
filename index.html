Getting to Cardney Steading

Cardney Steading is an hour and a half drive from Glasgow/Edinburgh and a ten minute drive from the nearest town, Dunkeld.

As its a rural road up to the venue we recommend booking a taxi, local firms include Dunkeld Cars and Lady Drive Taxi.

There is also parking at the venue and cars can be left overnight.
